GRIZZ


The oldest of the three brothers, Grizz is a caring big brother to Panda and Ice bear.

He loves action films, and likes to make his own movies where he plays a Spy by the name of "Crowbar Jones."

His skills include eating 30 burritos in 15 minutes, climbing trees in short amounts of time, and letting out an intimidating roar when needed.

Grizz is voiced by Eric Edelstein.

As a bear cub Grizz was found stuck in a tree by a fireman.
