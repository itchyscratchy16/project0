Ice bear

Ice Bear is truly a jack of all trades, as he possesses the apparent natural ability to do many skills one would need to practice for years in order to accomplish.

Ice Bear is no ordinary polar bear.

His sense of smell is truly strong as he was able to smell the police at his house that was over short distance away.

In the episode "Coffee Cave", Ice Bear is shown to have extraordinary baristing skills in which he can make great coffee drinks with various shapes of cream from animal, star to even a life-size cream statue in the coffee cup.

Ice Bear is voiced by Demetri Martin.

Ice Bear speaks fluent Russian.
